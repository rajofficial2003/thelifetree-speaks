FAQ

Common Questions

We believe informed patients make empowered decisions. Here are answers to the most frequent questions we receive.

If you are under 35 and have been trying to conceive for 12 months without success (or 6 months if over 35), it is advisable to consult a fertility specialist. However, if you have known conditions like PCOS, endometriosis, or irregular periods, early consultation is always recommended.

Most initial fertility evaluations are non-invasive, involving blood tests and ultrasound scans. Treatments like IUI are minimally invasive and nearly painless. IVF involves injections and a minor egg retrieval procedure, which is done under light sedation. Our team ensures maximum comfort throughout.

An initial evaluation typically takes 1-2 weeks. A standard IUI cycle is 2-3 weeks, while an IVF cycle from stimulation to embryo transfer takes approximately 4-6 weeks. Your personalized timeline will be clearly communicated at your first consultation.

Absolutely. All consultations, test results, and treatment details are strictly confidential. We adhere to the highest standards of medical privacy, and your information is never shared without your explicit consent. You may also choose to consult under a preferred name for additional privacy.

Our IVF success rates are among the highest in the region, consistently exceeding national averages. Success rates vary by age, diagnosis, and individual circumstances. During your consultation, we will give you a personalized probability assessment based on your specific situation.

Yes. Male factor infertility accounts for 40–50% of all infertility cases. We have a dedicated andrology department offering comprehensive semen analysis, hormonal testing, genetic testing, and advanced treatments like TESA/PESA for azoospermia. Both partners are evaluated together for the best outcomes.

We believe world-class fertility care should be accessible. We offer transparent, all-inclusive pricing with no hidden fees. Flexible EMI options and insurance coordination are available. We also have special packages for multiple cycles to maximize your chances while managing costs.
